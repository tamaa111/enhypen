2023.05.26 (FRI) / EVENT [&] LIVE

From May 29th (Monday) to June 4th (Sunday), ENHYPEN will be aired at YUNIKA VISION in front of Seibu
Shinjuku Station!

■Thank you very much for your warm support to ENHYPEN.

To commemorate the release of the 4th mini-album "DARK BLOOD", ENHYPEN will be aired at Yunika Vision in front of Seibu Shinjuku Station from May 29th (Monday) to June 4th (Sunday).

Broadcast period:
May 29th (Mon) - June 4th (Sun)

Broadcast time:
7:00, 9:00, 11:00, 13:00, 15:00, 17:00, 19:00, 21:00 for about 18 minutes
*Time are subject to change without notice.

Broadcast contents:
5 music videos including the latest songs from the 4th mini album "DARK BLOOD" will be aired.

1. Bite Me
2.Future Perfect (Pass the MIC) (Japanese Ver.)
3. Parado XXX Invasion
4. Tamed-Dashed (Japanese Ver.)
5.Blessed-Cursed

*The song is an uncut full version.
*Broadcast contents are subject to change.

(About the app "VISION α" interlocking project)
If you hold up your smartphone with the "VISION α" app downloaded to Yunika Vision while the program is airing, you will hear the program's audio from your smartphone in high-quality sound.
Put on your earphones and headphones and enjoy an immersive music and video experience!
